We're getting hitched!

Please celebrate with us for a long and relaxing weekend at Sugarloaf Ski Resort in Carrabassett Valley, Maine from Thursday, April 10th to Sunday, April 13th. We will ski, snowboard, enjoy a catered dinner with close family and friends and more. The annual Reggae Festival will be in full effect with good vibes, live music, and food trucks during our stay.

How we met

Max and Erica met at the University of Maine in 2018. Max was studying computer science and Erica was studying economics and math. They met through Tinder when Max asked to take Erica up for an airplane ride. Erica said no, telling Max her mother told her not to get into a stranger's airplane. Instead, they ran an errand together then watched The Office. They hit it off immediately, finding common interests in snowboarding, life experiences, ambition, and humor. Today, they live on a small farm with their dog, cats, and horses. Their love for one another grows stronger each day.
